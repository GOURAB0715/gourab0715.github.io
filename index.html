About Me

Hello, I am a PhD scholar in Computer Science at Shiv Nadar Institute of Eminence (Delhi-NCR), with a strong academic foundation and a deep interest in algorithms and theoretical aspects of computer science. I hail from Kabilpur, a small village in the North 24 Parganas district of West Bengal, which has shaped my grounded outlook and perseverance in pursuing higher education and research. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Centenary College, Rahara, followed by an M.Sc. in Computer Science from Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur. My education at Ramakrishna Mission institutions has instilled in me a disciplined approach to learning, ethical values, and a strong inclination toward academic rigor. Currently, my research focuses on algorithm design and analysis, and I am keen on exploring challenging problems that lie at the intersection of theory and efficient computation. I aspire to contribute to impactful research, collaborate with fellow researchers, and grow as an academic and problem solver.
